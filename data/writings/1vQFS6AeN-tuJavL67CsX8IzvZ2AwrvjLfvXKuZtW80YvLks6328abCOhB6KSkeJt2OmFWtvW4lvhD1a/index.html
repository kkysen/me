Book 3: Chapter 5

Surreal Catholic Heaven Dream

Sung to the tune of “Spooky Mormon Hell Dream” from The Book of Mormon

Lord Marchmain

Long ago when I was five,

I snuck in the kitchen late at night

And drank a beer without a maple glaze

My father asked who drank the beer

I said I was sorry (I wasn’t)

And my father gave me another beer

I’ve lived with cheating the system all of my life

And the amazing dream that I had that night

Yes, please!  I’d love to do it again!

Angels

Up, up thy soul is lifted!

From the earth whenceforth ye rose!

The path of light leads thee

To surreal Catholic Heaven dream!

You are having a surreal Catholic Heaven dream now!

Lord Marchmain

And now I’ve managed to do it again / Rectus!

I’ve had the Last Sacrament and tricked God / Discipuli!

I’ve left my hopeless Catholic guilt-ridden family behind and ascended to Heaven / Paradisiactus!

Oh God, how could I have fooled you? / Deus!

How could I deceive Ephesians 1:13[1]  / Arcadiactus!

And now my soul has risen once again

Back into surreal Catholic Heaven dream!

Angels

Up, up to God’s realm

See where you belong!

There is nothing you can do

No life of sin that could kick you out of surreal Catholic Heaven Dream!

Jesus

You got away with drinking a beer with your father,

And now you’ve lived a life of sin but got to Heaven by just accepting the last rites?!

You’re a SAINT!

Lord Marchmain

Jesus, I wasn’t that great!

Angels

Jesus loves you, this we know!

For Jesus just told you so!

You remember God?!

He is even more gullible!

God

Servants of God, have you heard the news?

Alexander absolved his long life of sin by just accepting the last rites!

Now he’s back with all you ex agnostics and atheist!

It’s super surreal-really!

Lord Marchmain

Thank you, Lord, it was such a clever decision by me

To reject you all my life and then change my mind at the last second,

Please I’d love to stay forever

In this surreal Catholic Heaven Dream

Angels

Surreal Catholic Heaven dream!

Saint Augustine, the Duke of Wellington, Shakespeare, Alexander Fleming, and Evelyn Waugh

The spirits all surround you!

Surreal, surreal, surrreeeallly!

Saint Augustine

I brought the grace of God to Christianity!

The Duke of Wellington

I defeated Napoleon and saved England!

Shakespeare

I was the greatest writer in the English language and my works have transcended time and space!

Alexander Fleming

I invented antibiotics and saved millions of lives!

Evelyn Waugh

I wrote a really long book about homosexuality that somehow spouted Catholic propaganda!

Lord Marchmain

You think that’s great.  I followed Ephesians 1:13! (*gasps*)

I fooled God!  I’m so much greater than you!

I love this surreal Catholic Heaven dream!

(Surreal Catholic Heaven dream!)

Oh, Heavenly Father!

All you had to do was give me one last chance

And I was able to fool you into bringing me to Heaven!

I can’t believe Jesus called me a SAINT!

Angels

Welcome, welcome to surreal Catholic Heaven dream!

You are never waking up from surreal Catholic Heaven dream!

Lord Marchmain and Angels

Oh please, Heavenly Father! / Up, up thy soul is lifted

Please let me sleep forever! / From the earth whenceforth you rose

All you had to do was give me one last chance / This must be it, you must be there

Et iam in Arcadia ego sum[2]! / You must be in surreal Catholic Heaven dream now!

Spooky Catholic Guilt Dream

Sung to the tune of “Spooky Mormon Hell Dream” from The Book of Mormon

Julia Flyte

Long ago when I was fifteen,

I snuck outside late at night

And dreamed of “an English diplomat of great but not very virile beauty,”[3]

“He was old, thirty-two or -three, and had been recently and tragically widowed”[4]

“A man a little subdued by earlier grief”[5]

But with “a great career before him”[6] and “listless in his loneliness”[7]

“A mild agnosticism himself”[8]

“A liking for the shows of religion”[9]

And “perfectly agreeable to having his children brought up Catholic”[10]

Charles asked who would chase such a man

I said that it was not him, it was Rex Mottram

And we got married later that year

I’ve lived with “a tiny bit of [a human being], unnaturally developed”

“A tiny bit of a man pretending he was the whole”[11] all of my life

And the terrible vision that I had that night

No, please!  I don’t wanna go back!

Demons

Down, down thy soul is cast!

From the earth whenceforth ye fell!

The path of fire leads thee

To spooky Catholic guilt dream!

Welcome back to spooky Catholic guilt dream!

You are having a spooky Catholic guilt dream now!

Julia Flyte

And now I’ve gone and done it again / Rectus!

I committed another awful sin / Dominus!

I left my husband and fucked Charles / Guiltytus!

Oh God, how could I have betrayed God’s grace? / Deus!

How could I break Commandment number seven? / Damnytus!

And now my soul has just been thrown

Back into spooky Catholic guilt dream!

Jesus

You married a divorced Protestant

And now after you went through all that to marry Rex,

You commit adultery with Charles to lead a long life full of sin

You’re a SLUT!

Julia Flyte

Jesus, I’m sorry!

Demons

Jesus hates you, this we know!

For Jesus just told you so!

You remember Lucifer?!

He is even spookier!

Lucifer

Minions of Hades, have you heard the news?

Julia was caught cheating on Rex

Now she’s back with all you cheating Protestants and hypocritical Catholics like Evelyn Waugh

It’s super spooky-wooky!

Julia Flyte

I’m sorry, Lord, it was selfish of me

To follow Charles into a life of sin,

Please, I don’t wanna be

In this spooky Catholic guilt dream!

Demons

Spooky Catholic guilt dream!

Henry VIII, Genghis Khan, Joseph Smith, Evelyn Waugh

The spirits all surround you!

Spooky, spooky, spooooky!

Henry VIII

I had six wives—divorce, execution, death, divorce, execution, and then I died—and I broke from the Roman Catholic Church just to marry a woman I later executed!

Genghis Khan

I raped so many women and killed so many men that chances are, you’re my descendant[12]!

Joseph Smith

I founded The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints and allowed polygyny!

Evelyn Waugh

I fucked three men[13] in Oxford, wrote a book about them, and then divorced Evelyn Waugh!

Julia Flyte

You think that’s bad?  I broke the Seventh Commandment! (*gasps*)

I left my husband and fucked my brother’s best friend!  I’m way worse than you!

I hate this spooky Catholic guilt dream!

(Spooky Catholic guilt dream!)

Oh, Heavenly Father!

Please give me one more chance!

I’ll dump Charles after he leaves his wife so that I can repent and begin the Operation of Grace!

I won’t break the rules again!

I can’t believe Jesus called me a SLUT!

Demons

Welcome, welcome to spooky Catholic guilt dream!

You are never waking up from spooky Catholic guilt dream!

Julia Flyte

Oh, please, Heavenly Father! / Down, down thy soul is cast

Please let me wake up! / From the earth whenceforth ye fell

Give me one more chance! / This must be it, you must be there

I won’t let you and Evelyn Waugh down again! You must be in spooky Catholic guilt dream now!

I’ll be a paragon of the Operation of Grace!

Quomodo tantum cum Deo sedeo sola ego![14]

I Repent

Sung to the tune of “I Believe” from The Book of Mormon

Julia Flyte

Ever since I was a child, I had “a face of flawless Florentine quattrocento beauty”[15]...

So what happened?

My family and friends all said I was the most beautiful...

So what happened?

I was supposed to marry “an English diplomat of great but not very virile beauty”[16]...

So what happened?

It was supposed to be all so exciting, to be living a life of love with Rex Mottram

But I allowed my faith to be shaken–oh, what’s the matter with me?

I’ve always longed to be a good Catholic, to live a life with God

This was the time for me to stay faithful, so then why was I so scared?

A husband who “simply wasn’t all there,”[17] “not a complete human being at all”[18]

And Charles Ryder “was making [his] first entry as the freeholder of a property [he] would enjoy and develop at leisure”[19]

What’s so sinful about that?

I must trust that my lord is mightier and always has my back

Now I must be completely devout; I can’t have even one shred of doubt!

I believe that the Lord God told us we must repent for our sins

I believe that in “what is theologically termed ‘the operation of Grace,’ that is to say, the unmerited and unilateral act of love by which God continually calls souls to Himself”[20]

I believe that I must repent for my life of sin, for committing adultery

I am a Catholic

And a Catholic just repents!

You cannot just repent for some sins, you have to repent for everything

My problem was “set[ting] up a rival good to God’s”[21], instead of repenting

“I [must] give up this one thing I want so much, however bad I am”[22]

Time to repent and ensure “he won’t quite despair of me in the end”[23]!

I believe that God has an Operation of Grace for all of us

I believe that operation involves me repenting for “living in sin, with sin, always the same, like an idiot child carefully nursed, guarded from the world”[24]

And I believe that the current pope, Pius XII, signed the Reichskonkordat with the Nazis that gave Hitler legitimacy

I am a Catholic!

And, Jesus, Mary and Joseph, a Catholic just repents! / A Catholic just repents!

I know that I must go and do the things my God commands / Things my God commands!

I realize now why “I can’t marry...Charles; [why] I can’t be with [him] ever again”[25]!

If you ask the Lord in faith, he will always answer you

Just repent for him and “be...punished again”[26]

Ms. Evelyn Waugh

We have an intruder!  My husband just walked right into camp!

His Operation of Grace is an even larger invasion than Operation Barbarossa

Trumping even Russia’s great patriotic call to Operation Bagration

As God calls all, even atheist, souls to Himself

Julia Flyte

I believe that Satan is tempting me with you

I believe that “the worse I am, the more I need God”[27]

And I believe that in 1945 God changed his mind about gay people! / Gay people!

You can be a Catholic!

A Catholic who just repents…

Charles Ryder

The fuck is this?  “I hope your heart...breaks”[28]

Julia Flyte

And now I can feel the excitement: this is the moment I was born to return to God’s grace

And I feel so incredible sharing my Catholic propaganda with you while Jews are suffering through the Holocaust

Evelyn Waugh says that if you read his book, if you look for the grace of God himself, you’ll repent

But you must promise him you “can’t be with [the One] ever again,”[29] and let your grace grow! / Let your grace grow!

I believe that God lives in a house called Brideshead!

I believe that Jesus had his own chapel as well!

And I believe that the Garden of Eden was in a monastery in Tunis!

If you repent, the Lord will grant you grace

And you’ll know it’s all worth it—you’ll just feel it

You’ll be a Catholic!

And, by gosh, a Catholic just repeeeents! / A Catholic just repents!

/ Just repent, a Catholic just repeeeents

Oh, I repeeeent! / Just repent, a Catholic just…

I repeeeent! / ...repeeeent!
